White Sauce Pasta Recipe

Description

Hello, pasta lovers. You never need to make box mac and cheese again. Enter this basic White Sauce Pasta! This is a staple recipe in our house. The basic cream sauce is so creamy and full of flavor, and get this: it doesn't even use cream! All you need is a handful of ingredients you probably already have on hand. It takes just 10 minutes: enough time to make the pasta. Eat it straight up, as a pasta side dish, or add more raw or cooked veggies to the sauce. A quick dinner idea made in minutes!

Ingredients

  • Main Ingredients:

    • Pasta of any shape
    • Salted butter
    • Garlic
    • Flour
    • Milk
    • Salt
    • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Measure out all the ingredients first, and don't leave the stove! The cooking process goes quickly, so you don't want to have to stop to measure an ingredient. Don't get distracted, especially in the first few minutes!
  2. Saute the garlic only 30 seconds. Be careful not to overcook: you don't want it to brown!
  3. Add flour and whisk constantly. The flour can overcook in an instant, turning a brown color. Only cook it until it's golden.
  4. Whisk in the milk gradually. Add just a little milk, then whisk it in. Continue adding little bits until the sauce is smooth. Then cook about 5 minutes until thickened, and add the Parmesan and salt. Voila!
